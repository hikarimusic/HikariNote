Thorax 
======

Thoracic Wall
-------------

Skeleton
^^^^^^^^

- **Sternum**

  - **Feature**: *flat bone, shorter & thinner in female*
  - **Manubrium**

    - **Feature**: *jugular notch, clavicular notch*
    - **Articulation**: *clavicle, 1st rib, 2nd rib, body of sternum (manubriosternal joint)*

  - **Sternal angle (of Louis)**

    - **Junction**: *between manubrium & body of sternum*
    - **Level**: 
    
      - **A**: *2nd rib articulate with sternum*
      - **B**: *aortic arch begin & end*
      - **C**: *trachea bifurcate*
      - **D**: *inferior border of superior mediastinum*
      - **E**: *intervertebral disk T4-T5*

  - **Body of sternum**

    - **Articulation**: *2nd-7th costal cartilage, xiphoid process (xiphisternal joint)*

  - **Xiphoid process**

    - **Feature**: *cartilaginous process at birth, ossify after middle age*
    - **Level**: *xiphisternal joint (T9), xiphoid process (T10)*

      - **A**: *lower limit of heath & thoracic cage*
      - **B**: *upper limit of liver & diaphragm*
    
    - **Attachment**: *linea alba*

- **Rib**

  - **Feature**: *12 pairs, form thoracic cage, from vertebrae to sternum*
  - **Movement**: *increase anteroposterior & transverse diameter*
  - **Structure**

    - **Typical rib**: *rib 3-9, head, neck, tubercle, body*
    - **Head**: *articulate with vertebral body (corresponding & supraadjacent) & intervertebral disk*
    - **Tubercle**: *articulate with transverse process of vertebrae*
    - **Body**: *turn anteriorly at angle*
    - **Costal groove**: *inferior & internal surface, lodge intercostal vessel & nerve*

  - **Classification**

    - **True rib**: *rib 1-7, attach to sternum (through costal cartilage)*
    - **False rib**: *rib 8-12, attach to costal cartilage above & form costal arch (rib 8-10)*
    - **Floating rib**: *rib 11-12, attach only to vertbrae*

  - **First rib**

    - **Feature**: *broadest & shortest*
    - **Articulation**: *single facet*
    - **Scalene tubercle**: *insertion of anterior scalene*
    - **Two Grooves**: *subclavian vein & artery*

  - **Second rib**

    - **Feature**: *two facet*

  - **Tenth rib**

    - **Feature**: *single facet*

  - **Eleventh & twelfth rib**

    - **Feature**: *single facet, no neck & tubercle*

Articulation 
^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Sternoclavicular joint**: *between sternum & clavicle, synovial joint*
- **Sternocostal joint**: *between sternum & costal cartilage, 1st rib: synchondrosis, 2nd-7th rib: synovial joint*
- **Costochondral joint**: *beetween rib & costal cartilage, synchondrosis*
- **Manubriosternal joint**: *between manubrium & body of sternum, symphysis*
- **Xiphisternal joint**: *between xiphoid process & body of sternum, synchondrosis*
- **Costovertebral joint**: *between head of rib & vetebral body, synovial joint*
- **Costotransverse joint**: *between tubercle of rib & transverse process of vertebra, synovial joint*
- **Interchondral joint**: *between 6-10th costal cartilage, synovial joint*

Breast and Mammary Gland 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Breast**

  - **Composition**: *mammary gland, fibrous & fatty tissue, vessel & nerve*
  - **Position**: *2nd-6th rib, sternum to midaxillary line*
  - **Division**: *upper lateral quadrant, upper medial quadrant, lower lateral quadrant, lower medial quadrant*
  - **Fascia**: *mammary gland in superficial fascia*
  - **Ligament**: *suspensory ligament (of Cooper), from skin to deep layer of superficial fascia*
  - **Nipple**: *4th intercostal space, smooth muscle contract on tactile*
  - **Areola**: *ring of pigmented skin*
  - **Vasculature**: *internal thoracic artery (medial mammary branch), lateral thoracic artery (lateral mammary branch), posterior intercostal artery (lateral cutaneous branch)*
  - **Innervation**: *intercostal nerve (anterior & lateral cutaneous branch)*
  - **Abnormality**: *more breast or nipple, no breast or nipple*

- **Mammary gland**

  - **Structure**: *modified sweat gland*
  - **Axillary tail**: *part extending superolaterally*
  - **Retrimammary space**: *separate breast from deep fascia of muscle*
  - **Lobe**: *15-20 lobe, with lactiferous duct & lactiferous sinus*

- **Lymphatic**

  - **Axillary node (pectoral)**: *from lateral quadrant & nipple, primary*
  - **Parasternal node**: *from medial quadrant*
  - **Other**: *to apical axillary node, to abdominal wall*

Muscle 
^^^^^^

- **External intercostal**

  - **Origin**: *rib (lower border)*
  - **Insertion**: *rib below (upper border)*
  - **Nerve**: *intercostal*
  - **Action**: *elevate rib*
  
- **Internal intercostal**

  - **Origin**: *rib (lower border)*
  - **Insertion**: *rib below (upper border)*
  - **Nerve**: *intercostal*
  - **Action**: *costal part: depress rib, interchondral part: elevate rib*
  
- **Innermost intercostal**

  - **Origin**: *rib (lower border)*
  - **Insertion**: *rib below (upper border)*
  - **Nerve**: *intercostal*
  - **Action**: *elevate rib*
  
- **Transversus thoracic**

  - **Origin**: *sternum (posterior surface)*
  - **Insertion**: *costal cartilage (inner surface)*
  - **Nerve**: *intercostal*
  - **Action**: *depress rib*
  
- **Subcostalis**

  - **Origin**: *rib (inner surface near angle)*
  - **Insertion**: *rib 2-3 below (upper border)*
  - **Nerve**: *intercostal*
  - **Action**: *elevate rib*
  
- **Levator costarum**

  - **Origin**: *C7-T11 vertebra (transverse process)*
  - **Insertion**: *rib below (tubercle)*
  - **Nerve**: *C8-T11 (posterior rami)*
  - **Action**: *elevate rib*

Nerve and Blood Vessel 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Intercostal nerve**

  - **Origin**: *T1-T11 spinal nerve (anterior ramim), T12: subcostal nerve*
  - **Position**: *between internal & innermost intercostal, Vein-Artery-Nerve from top to down*
  - **Lodge**: *costal groove (inferior surface of rib)*
  - **Branch**: *lateral cutaneous branch, anterior cutaneous branch, muscular branch*

- **Internal thoracic artery**

  - **Origin**: *subclavian artery (1st part)*
  - **Course**: *descend behind costal cartilage, divide into musculophrenic artery & superior epigastric artery*
  - **Pericardiophrenic artery**

    - **Course**: *acompany phrnic nerve, between pleura & pericardium, to diaphragm*
    - **Supply**: *pleura, pericardium, diaphragm*

  - **Anterior intercostal artery**

    - **Course**: *2 in each intercostal space*
    - **Upper artery**: *anastomose with posterior intercostal artery*
    - **Lower artery**: *anastomose with posterior intercostal artery (collateral branch)*
    - **Supply**: *intercostal, serratus anterior, pectoral muscle*

  - **Anterior perforating branch**

    - **Course**: *perforate intercostal muscle, course with anterior cutaneous branch of intercostal nerve*
    - **Supply**: *pectoralis major, skin & subcutaneous tissue*
    - **Branch**: *medial mammary branch*

  - **Musculophrenic artery**

    - **Course**: *follow costal arch (inner surface), give rise to 2 artery in 7th-9th intercostal space, pierce diaphragm, anastomose with deep circumfex iliac artery*
    - **Supply**: *pericardium, diaphragm, muscle of anterior abdominal wall*

  - **Superior epigastric atery**

    - **Course**: *descend deep to rectus abdominus, anastomuse with inferior epigastric artery*
    - **Supply**: *diaphragm, peritoneum, anterior abdominal wall*

- **Internal thoracic vein**

  - **Empty**: *brachiocephalic vein*

Lymphatic
^^^^^^^^^

- **Parasternal node**

  - **Position**: *along internal thoracic artery**
  - **Recieve**: *breast (medial portion), intercostal space, diaphragm, supraumbillical region*
  - **Empty**: *junction of internal jugular vein & subclavian vein*

- **Intercostal node**

  - **Position**: *near head of rib*
  - **Recieve**: *intercostal space, pleura*
  - **Empty**: *cisterna chyli, thoracic duct*

- **Phrenic node**

  - **Position**: *thoracic surface of diaphragm*
  - **Recieve**: *pericardium, diaphragm, liver*
  - **Empty**: *parasternal node, posterior mediastinal node*

Diaphragm 
^^^^^^^^^

- **Diaphragm**

  - **Origin**: *xiphoid process, lower costal cartilage, lumbocostal arch, L1-L3 vertebra*
  - **Insertion**: *central tendon*
  - **Nerve**

    - **Motor**: *phrenic nerve*
    - **Sensory**: *central: phrenic nerve, peripheral: intercostal nerve*

  - **Vasculature**: *musculophrenic artery, pericardiophrenic artery, superior phrenic artery, inferior phrenic artery*
  - **Action**: *contract: descend & increase thoracic volume, relax: ascend & decrease thoracic volume*
  - **Function**: *principle muscle of inspiration*
  - **Structure**

    - **Crus**: *right crus larger than left crus, originate from L1-L3 vertebra, split to enclose esophagus*
    - **Medial arcyate ligament**: *from body to transverse process of L1 vertebra, pass overe psoas muscle & sympathetic trunk*
    - **Lateral arcuate ligament**: *from transverse process of L1 to rib 12, pass over quadratus lumborum*

- **Aperture**

  - **Vena caval hiatus**

    - **Position**: *in central tendon, level of T8*
    - **Transmit**: *inferior vena cava, (right phrenic nerve)*

  - **Esophageal hiatus**

    - **Position**: *in muscular part (right crus), level of T10*
    - **Transmit**: *esophagus, anterior & posterior vagus nerve*

  - **Aortic hiatus**

    - **Position**: *between two crura, level of T12*
    - **Transmit**: *aorta, thoracic duct, azygos vein, (greater splanchnic nerve)*

Lung and Pleura 
---------------

Trachea and Bronchi 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Trachea**

  - **Begin**: *continuation of larynx, at inferior border of cricoid cartilage (C6)*
  - **End**: *bifurcate into right & left main bronchi, at level of sternal angle (T4-T5)*
  - **Cartilage**: *16-20 imcomplete hyaline cartiliginous ring, open posteiorly, prevent trachea from collapsing*
  - **Carina**

    - **Position**: *downward projection of last tracheal cartilage, ridge separate right & left main bronchus*
    - **Physiology**: *mucous membrane most sensitive, associated with cough reflex*
    - **Disease**: *distorted & immobile in presence of bronchogenic carcinoma*

- **Right main bronchus**

  - **Structure**: *shorter, wider, more vertical (more foreign body enter)*
  - **Position**: *inferior to arch of azygos vein, anterior to esophagus, posterior to superior vena cava*
  - **Lobar (secondary) bronchi**: *superior (eparterial), middle, inferior (hyparterial)*
  - **Segmental bronchi**: *10 segmental bronchi*

- **Left main bronchus**

  - **Position**: *inferior to arch of aorta, anteriro to esophagus & thoracic aorta*
  - **Lobar (secondary) bronchi**: *upper, lower*
  - **Segmental bronchi**: *8-10 segmental bronchi*

Pleura and Pleural Cavity 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Pleura**

  - **Parietal pleura**

    - **Position**: *line inner surface of thoracic wall & mediastinum*
    - **Part**: *costal, diaphragmatic, mediastinal, cervical*
    - **Cervical pleura (cupula)**: *project into neck above 1st rib, reinforced by suprapleural membrane (Sibson fascia) (attach to 1st rib & C7 vertebra)*
    - **Endothoracic fascia**: *separate thoracic wall & pleura*
    - **Innrevation**: *very sensitive to pain*

      - **Intercostal nerve**: *costal pleura, peripheral diaphragmatic pleura*
      - **Phernic nerve**: *mediastinal pleura, central diaphragmatic pleura*

    - **Vasculature**: 
    
      - **Artery**: *internal thoracic artyer, superior phrenic artery, posterior intercostal artery, superior intercostal artery*
      - **Vein**: *to systemic vein*
    
    - **Pulmonary ligament**: *vertical fold of mediastinal pleura, extend from hilus to base, support lung in pleura sac*

  - **Visceral pleura**

    - **Position**: *invest lung, dip into fissure*
    - **Vasculature**

      - **Artery**: *bronchial artery*
      - **Vein**: *to pulmonary vein*
    
    - **Innrvation**: *insensitive to pain, sensitive to stretch, involved in respiratory reflex*

- **Pleural cavity**

  - **Structure**: *potential space between parietal & visceral pleura, closed sac (no communication between left & right sac), contain fluid for lumbrication*
  - **Costodiaphragmatic recess**: *reflection of costal & diaphragmatic pleura, can accumulate fluid, alow lung expand during inspiration*
  - **Costomediastinal recess**: *reflection of costal & mediastinal pleura*

Lung 
^^^^

- **Feature**

  - **Function**: *essential organ of respiration*
  - **Base**: *on surface of diaphragm, descend during inspirtion, ascend during expiration*
  - **Vasculature (nonrespiratory)**: 
  
    - **Artery**: *bronchial artery*
    - **Vein**: *bronchial vein (larger division of bronchial tree), pulmonary vein (smaller division of bronchial tree)*

  - **Innervation**

    - **Parasympathetic fiber**: *smooth muscle (bronchoconstrictor), gland (secretomotor)*
    - **Sympathetic fiber**: *smooth muscle (bronchodilator), blood vessel (vasoconstrictor)*
    - **Sensory ending**: *vagal origin, stinulated by stretching of lung, reflex control of respiration*

- **Right lung**

  - **Structure**: *larger & heavier, shorter & wider (high diaphragm & left heart)*
  - **Fissure**: *oblique fissure (follow 6th rib), horizontal fissure (from 6th rib to 4th costal cartilage)*
  - **Lobe**: *upper lobe, middle lobe, lower lobe*
  - **Bronchi**: *3 lobar bronchi, 10 segmental bronchi, (1 bronchial artery)*
  - **Feature**: *groove of SVC, groove of arch of azygos vein, groove of esophagus*

- **Left lung**

  - **Structure**: *more vertical*
  - **Fissure**: *obilque fissure (follow 6th rib)*
  - **Lobe**: *upper lobe, lower lobe*
  - **Bronchi**: *2 lobar bronchi, 8-10 segmental bronchi, (2 bronchi artery)*
  - **Lingula**: *tongue-shaped portion of upper lobe*
  - **Feature**: *cardiac impression, cardiac notch, groove of aortic arch, groove of descending aorta, groove of left subclavian artery*

- **Bronchopulmonary  segment**

  - **General**: *anatomic unit, functional unit, surgical removable unit*
  - **Content**: *a segment bronchus, a segmental artery, a segment of lung tissue, surrounded by intersegmental septum*
  - **Vasculature**: *segmental bronchus & artery: intrasegmental, pulmonary vein: intersegmental*

- **Airway**

  - **Conducting portion**: *nasal cavity, pharynx, larynx, trachea, bronchi, bronchiole, terminal bronchiole*
  - **Respiratory portion**: *respiratory bronchiole, alveolar duct, atria, alveolar sac*

Respiration 
^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Inspiration**

  - **Muscle**: *diaphragm, external & internal (interchondral part) & innermost intercostal, sternocleidomastoid, levator sternum, serratus anterior, scalene, pectoralis major & minor, serratus posterior superior*
  - **Process**

   - **Contraction of diaphragm**: *increase vertical diameter of thorax*
   - **Enlargement of lung**: *reduce intrapulmonary pressure*
   - **Forced inspiratoin**: *contraction of intercostal muscle, elevation of rib & sternum, increase transverse & anteroposterior diameter of thorax*

- **Expiration**

  - **Muscle**: *muscle of anterior abdominal wall, internal intercostal (costal part), serratus posterior inferior*
  - **Process**

    - **Overall process**: *relaxation of diaphragm & intercostal muscle, decrease thoracic volume*
    - **Elactic recoil of lung**: *increase intrapulmonary pressure*
    - **Forced expiration**: *contraction of anterior abdominal muscle & internal intercostal (costal part)*

Blood Vessel 
^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Pulmonary trunk**

  - **Origin**: *conus arteriosus of right ventricle of heart*
  - **Course**: *pass superiorly & posteriorly, from front to left side of ascending aorta, bifurcate into left & right pulmonary artery (level of sternal angle)*
  - **Feature**: *carry deoxygenated blood to lung for oxygenation, blood pressure lower than aorta*
  - **Left pulmonary artery**
  
    - **Course**: *toward left lung, arch over left bronchus*
    - **Feature**: *shorter & narrower, connected to arch of aorta by ligamentum arteriosum (remain of ductus arteriorsus)*

  - **Right pulmonary artery**

    - **Course**: *toward right lung, under arch of aorta, posterior to ascending arota & SVC, anterior to right bronchus*

- **Pulmonary vein**

  - **Feature**: *intersegmental, 5 from each lobe, 4 enter left atrium (right upper & middle vein join)*
  - **Recieve**: *alveoli (respiratory part, oxygenated), visceral pleura & bronchiole (nonrespiratory, deoxygenated)*
  - **Empty**: *left atrium of heart*

- **Bronchial artery**

  - **Origin**: *thoracic aorta, 1 for right lung, 2 for left lung*
  - **Supply**: *nonrespiratory conducting tissue, visceral pleura, anastomose with pulmonary system*

- **Bronchial vein**

  - **Recieve**: *bronchi, (tracheobronchial lymph node)*
  - **Empty**: *azygos vein & accessory hemiazygos vein (superior intercostal vein)*

Lymphatic 
^^^^^^^^^

- **Drain**: *bronchial tree, pulmonary vessel, septa, no pulmonary alveoli*
- **Course**: *toward hilus, pulmonary node, bronchopulmonary node, tracheobronchial node, tracheal node, bronchomediastinal node & trunk, thoracic duct & right lymphatic duct*

Nerve 
^^^^^

- **Pulmonary plexus**

  - **Recieve**: *vagus nerve, sympathetic trunk, cardiac plexus*
  - **Position**: *anterior pulmonary plexus: anterior to root of lung, posterior pulmonary plexus: posterior to root of lung*
  - **Branch**: *accopany vessel & bronchi into lung*
  - **Function**

    - **Sympathetic**: *dilate bronchi, constrict pulmonary vessel*
    - **Parasympathetic**: *constrict bronchi, dilate pulmonary vessel, increase glandular secretion*

- **Phrenic nerve**

  - **Origin**: *C3-C5 spinal nerve*
  - **Course**: *enter thorax (between subclavian vein & artery), run anterior to root of lung (vagus posterior to root of lung), descend between mediastinal pleura & pericardium (accompany pericardiophrenic vessel)*
  - **Innervate**: 
  
    - **Motor**: *diaphragm*
    - **Sensory**: *fibrous pericardium, mediastinal & diaphragmatic pleura, central diaphragm*

Heart and Pericardium 
---------------------

Pericardium 
^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Pericardium**

  - **General**: *enclose heart & root of great vessel, occupy middle mediastinum*
  - **Component**: *fibrous pericardium, serous pericardium*
  - **Vasculature**: *pericardiophrenic artery, bronchial artery, esophageal artery*
  - **Innervation**: *phrenic nerve, vagus nerve, sumpathetic trunk*

- **Fibrous pericardium**: *blend with adventitia or great vessel root & central tendon of diaphrgm*
- **Serous pericardium**

  - **Parietal layer**: *inner surface of fibrous pericardium*
  - **Visceral layer**: *outer layer of heart (epicardium) & gret vessel root*

- **Pericardial cavity**: *potential space between visceral & parietal layer of serous pericardium*
- **Pericardial sinus**

  - **Transverse sinus**: *subdivision of pericardial sac, posterior to ascending aorta & pulmonary trunk, anterior to SVC, superior to left atrium & pulmonary artery, surgeon make ligature through sinus to stop circulation*
  - **Oblique sinus**: *subdividion of pericardial sac, behind heart, reflection of pericardium around pulmonary vein & IVC*

Heart 
^^^^^

- **General feature**

  - **Apex**: *rounded extremity of heart, by left ventricle, in left 5th intrcostal space, 9cm from midline*
  - **Base**: *by left atrium*
  - **Border**

    - **Right (acute) border**: *by SVC, right atrium, IVC*
    - **Left (obtuse) border**: *by left ventricle*

  - **Layer**: *endocardium, myocardium, epicardium*
  - **Sulcus terminalis**: *groove on extrnal surface of right atrium, correspond to crista terminalis, between sinus venosus & atrium (embryo)*
  - **Coronary sulcus**: *groove on external surface of heart, between atria & ventricles*
  - **Cardiovascular silhouette**: 
  
    - **Feature**: *contour of heart & great vessel on posterior-anterior chest radiograph*
    - **Right border**: *SVC, right atrium, IVC*
    - **Left border**: *aortic arch (aortic knob), pulmonary trunk, left atrium, left ventricle*

- **Internal Structure**

  - **Right atrium**

    - **Wall**: *anterior rough atrium proper (with pectinate muscle), posterior smooth sinus venarum (with 2 vena cava openings), separateed by crista terminalis*
    - **Size**: *largr & thinner than left atrium*
    - **Pressue**: *lower than left atrial pressure*
    - **Valve**: *valve of IVC, valve of coronary sinus*
    - **Right auricle**: *muscular pouch on upper anterior right atrium*
    - **Sinus venarum**: *smooth, separated from muscular atrium proper by crista terminais, from embryonic sinus venosus, recieve SVC & IVC & coronary sinus & anterior cardiac vein*
    - **Pectinate muscle**: *ridge of atrium myocardium*
    - **Crista terminalis**: *vertical muscular ridge, origin of pectinate muscle, junction between primitive sinus venarum & atrium proper, correspond to sulcus terminalis*
    - **Fossa ovalis**: *oval-shaped depression in interatrial septum, site of foramen ovale, upper margin called limbus fossa ovale*

  - **Left atrium**

    - **Wall**: *smooth*
    - **Size**: *smaller & thicker than right atrum*
    - **Position**: *most posterior of four chamber, anterior to esopahgus*
    - **Recieve**: *pulmonary vein*

  - **Right ventricle**

    - **Position**: *anterior of heart*
    - **Trabeculae carnae cordis**: *ridge of myocardium of ventricle*
    - **Papillary muscle**

      - **Structure**: *cone-shaped muscle, from vectricular wall & septum to chordae tendineae*
      - **FUnction**: *tighten chordae tendineae, prevent eversion of tricuspid valve & reguigitation*
      
    - **Chordae tendineae**: *from papillary muscle to cusp of tricuspid valve, prevent eversion of valve*
    - **Conus arteriosus**: *upper smooth portion of right ventricle, lead to pulmonary trunk*
    - **Septomarginal trabecula (moderator band)**

      - **Structure**: *band of trabeculae carnae between intraventricular septum & base of pupillary muscle*
      - **Function**: *prevent overdistention of ventricle, carry right limb of atrioventricular bundle*

    - **Intraventricular septum**: *mostly muscular, small membranous part*

  - **Left ventricle**

    - **Apex**: *apex downward & forward & toward left*
    - **Aortic vestibule**: *upper anterior part, lead to aorta*
    - **Component**: *papillary muscle, chordae tendineae, trabeculae carnae cordis*
    - **Wall**: *thicker & longer & narrower than right ventricle, perform harder work*

- **Heart valve**

  - **Pulmonary valve**

    - **Position**: *behind left 3rd costal cartilage*
    - **Motion**: *open by ventricular systole, close by ventricular diastole (slightly after clousure of aortic valve)*
    - **Audible**: *left 2nd intercostal space, lateral to sternum*

  - **Aortic valve**

    - **Position**: *behind left sternum at 3rd intercostal space*
    - **Motion**: *open by ventricular systole, close by ventricular diastole*
    - **Audible**: *right 2nd intrcostal space, lateral to sternum*

  - **Tricuspid valve**

    - **Position**: *between right atrium & ventricle, behind right sternum at 4th intercostal space (slightly after clousure of mitral valve)*
    - **Cusp**: *anterior & posterior & septal cusp*
    - **Motion**: *close by ventricular systole*
    - **Audible**: *right (left) lower part of body of sternum*

  - **Bicuspid valve**

    - **Position**: *between left atrium & ventricle, behind left sternum at 4th costal cartilage*
    - **Cusp**: *anterior & posterior cusp (mitral valve)*
    - **Motion**: *close by ventricular systole*
    - **Audible**: *left 5th intercostal space, at midclavicular line*

- **Heart sound**

  - **First "lub" sound**: *closure of mitral & tricuspid valve, at onset of ventricular systole*
  - **Second "dub" sound**: *closure of aortic & pulmonary valve, at onset of ventricular diastole*

- **Conducting system**

  - **General**: *modified cardiac muscle beneath endocardium, carry impulse throughout cardiac muscle, signal chamber contract*
  - **Sinoatrial node**

    - **Position**: *right atrium (near opening of SVC), upper end of crista terminais*
    - **Function**: *pacemaker, intiate heartbeat, can be altered by autonomic stimulation (sympathetic speed up, parasympathetic speed down)*
    - **Impulse**: *spread in atria, travel along intrnodal pathway to AV node*
    - **Blood Supply**: *sinus node artery (branch of right coronary artery)*

  - **Atrioventricular node**

    - **Position**: *right atrium (superior to coronary sinus opening)*
    - **Function**: *recieve impulse from SA node, pass to AV bundle, innervated by autonomic nerve fiber*
    - **Blood supply**: *AV node artery (branch of right coronary artery)*

  - **Atrioventricular bundle (of His)**

    - **Course**: *from AV node, run along interventricular septum (membranous part)*
    - **Branch**: *split into right & left branch, descend into interventricular septum (muscular part), branch into purkinje fiber, spread into ventricular wall*

- **Coronary artery**

  - **Origin**: *ascending aorta, filled during ventricular diastole*
  - **Blood flow**: *maximal during diastole, minumal during systole (compression by myocardium)*
  - **Right coronary artery**
    
    - **Origin**: *right aortic sinus of ascending aorta (above aortic valve)*
    - **SA nodal artery**: *encircle base of SVC, supply SA node & right atrium*
    - **Branch from RCA**: *supply right ventricle* 
    - **Right Marginal artery**: *along inferior border, supply inferior of right ventricle*
    - **Posterior IV artery**: *in posterior interventricular sulcus, supply IV septum*
    - **AV nodeal artery**: *supply AV node*

  - **Left coronary artery**

    - **Origin**: *left aortic sinus of ascending aorta (above aortic valve)*
    - **Anterior IV artery**: *in anterior interventricular sulcus, supply IV septum*
    - **Circumflex artery**: *in coronary sulcus, supply left atrium & ventricle*
    - **Left marginal artery**: *from circumflex artery*

- **Cardiac vein & coronary sinus**

  - **Coronary sinus**

    - **Position**: *in coronary sinus, separate atria & ventricles**
    - **Recieve**: *great cardiac vein, middle cardiac vein, small cardiac vein, obique vein (left atrium), posterior vein (left ventricle)*
    - **Empty**: *right atrium, between opening of IVC & AV (have one-cusp valve)*

  - **Great cardiac vein**: *begin at apex of heart, ascend in anterior IV groove, turn left into coronary sinus, drain into coronary sinus*
  - **Middle cardiac vein**: *begin at apex of heart, ascend in posterior IV groove, drain into coronary sinus*
  - **Small cardiac vein**: *run along right margin of heart, turn into coronary sulcus, drain into coronary sinus*
  - **Oblique cardiac vein (posterior left atrium)**: *descend into coronary sinus*
  - **Anterior cardiac vein (anterior right ventricle)**: *drain into right atrium*
  - **Smallest cardiac vein (venae cordis minimae)**: *begin in wall of heart, empty directly into chamber*

- **Lymphatic**

  - **Recieve**: *myocardium, epicardium*
  - **Empty**: *anterior mediastinal node (right), tracheobronchial node (left)*

- **Cardiac plexus**

  - **Recieve**: *sympathetic trunk & vagus nerve (cardiac nerve)*
  - **Position**: *superficial cardiac plexus: beneath aortic arch, deep cardiac plexus: posterior to aortic arch & anterior to trachea bifurcation*
  - **Innervation**: *right branch: to SA node, left branch: to AV node, cardiac muscle: no ending*
  - **Function**

    - **Sympathetic**: *increase heart rate, dilate coronary artery*
    - **Parasympathetic**: *decrease heart rate, contrict coronary artery*

Great Vessel 
^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Ascending aorta**

  - **Course**: *from left ventricle, in middle mediastinum*
  - **Branch**: *right & left coronary artery (from aortic sinus above aortic valve)*

- **Arch of aorta**

  - **Course**: *continuation of ascending aorta, in superior mediastinum, arch over right pulmonary artery & left main bronchus*
  - **Branch**: *brachiocephalic artery, left common carotid artery, left subclavian artery*

- **Superior vena cave**

  - **Course**: *union of right & left brachiocephalic vein, descend right to ascneding aorta, revieve azygos vein, enter right atrium, in superior & middle mediastinum*

- **Pulmonary trunk**

  - **Course**: *from right ventricle, pass upward across origin and to left of ascending aorta, bifurcate into right & left pulmonary artery*

Mediastinum 
-----------

Mediastinum
^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Boundary**: *lateral: pleural cavity, anterior: sternum, posterior: vertebral column*
- **Composition**: *superior mediastinum, anterior mediastinum, middle mediastinum, posterior mediastinum*
- **Superior mediastinum**

  - **Boundary**: *superior: plane of first rib, inferior: plane from sternal angle to T4-T5 intevertebral disk*
  - **Content**: *thymus, phrnic nerve, branch of superior vena cava, branch of aortic arch, trachea, recurrent laryngeal nerve, esophagus, vagus verve, thoracic duct*

- **Anterior mediastinum**

  - **Boundary**: *anterior: sternum, posterior: pericardium*
  - **Content**: *thymus, fat & connective tissue*

- **Middle mediastinum**

  - **Boundary**: *between left & right pleural cavity*
  - **Content**: *heart, pericardium, phrenic nerve, root of great vessel, arch of azygos vein, main bronchi*

- **Posterior mediastinum**

  - **Boundary**: *anterior: pericardium, posterior: vertebral column*
  - **Content**: *esophagus, vagus nerve, thoracic aorta, azygos & hemiazygos vein, thoracic duct, sympathetic trunk, splanchnic nerve*

Thymus
^^^^^^

- **Position**: *superior mediastinum (anterior to trachea)*
- **Development**: *large in neonate, grow until puberty, involute & replaced by fat*
- **Function**: *maturation of T-lymphocyte precursor, procude thymosin*
- **Vasculature**: *inferior thyroid artery, internal thoracic artery*

Esophagus 
^^^^^^^^^

- **Position**: *from pharynx to stomach, descend behind trachea*
- **Constriction**

  - **Upper constriction**: *beginning of esophagus (level of cricoid cartilage)*
  - **Middle constriction**: *crossed by aortic arfch & left main bronchus*
  - **Inferiro constriction**: *esophageal hiatus (level of T10)*

- **Sphincter**: *physiologic phincter by circular smooth muscle, at gastroesophageal junctin (inferior esophageal sphincter)*
- **Vasculature**: *inferior thyroid artery, bronchial & esophageal artery, left inferior phrenic artery, left gastric artery*

Blood and Lymphatic Vessel 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Thoracic aorta**

  - **Course**: *begin at level of T4, descend left to vertebral column, pass through aortic hiatus of diaphragm*
  - **Posterior intercostal artery**

    - **1st-2nd intercostal artery**: *from supreme intercostal artery of costocervical trunk*
    - **3rd-11th intercostal artery**: *from thoracic aorta, have collateral branch*
    - **12th subcostal artery**: *from thoracic aorta*

  - **Other branch**: *pericardial branch, bronchial branch, esophageal branch, mediastinal branch, superior phrenic branch*

- **Azygos venous system**

  - **Azygos vein**

    - **Origin**: *union of right ascending lumbar vein & right subcostal vein, connected to inferior vena cava*
    - **Course**: *enter thorax (through aortic hiatus), ascend on right side of vertebral body*
    - **Recieve**: *right intercostal vein, right superior intercostal vein, hemiazygos vein, accessory hemiazygos vein*
    - **Empty**: *superior vena cava, arch over root of right lung*

  - **Hemiazygos vein**

    - **Origin**: *union of left ascending lumbar vein & left subcostal vein, connected to left renal vein*
    - **Course**: *ascend on left side of vertebral body*
    - **Recieve**: *9th-11th intercostal vein*
    - **Empty**: *azygos vein*

  - **Accessory hemiazygos vein**

    - **Origin**: *5th intercostal space*
    - **Course**: *descend on left side of vertebral body*
    - **Recieve**: *5th-8th intercostal vein*
    - **Empty**: *azygos vein*

  - **Superior intercostal vein**

    - **Origin**: *union of 2nd-3th intercostal vein*
    - **Empty**: *right: azygos vein, left: brachiocephalic vein*

  - **Posterior intercostal vein**

    - **1st intercostal vein**: *drain into brachiocephalic vein*
    - **2nd-4th intercostal vein**: *drain into superior intercostal vein*
    - **5th-12th intercostal vein**: *right: azygos vein, left: (accessory) hemiazygos vein*

- **Lymphatic**

  - **Thoracic duct**

    - **Origin**: *cisterna chyli (dilated junction abdominal lymphatic trunk)*
    - **Structure**: *beaded due to valve, often double or triple ducts*
    - **Recieve**: *lower limb, pelvis, abdomen, left thorax, left upper limb, left head & neck*
    - **Course**: *enter thorax (through aortic hiatus), ascend between azygos vein & aorta, arch laterally over left pleura, pass behind left internal jugular vein*
    - **Empty**: *junction of left internal jugular vein & subclavian vein*

  - **Right lymphatic duct**

    - **Recieve**: *right thorax, right upper limb, right head & neck*
    - **Empty**: *junctin of right internall jugular vein & subclavian vein*

Automatic nervous system 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Automatic nervous system**

  - **Function**: *efferent nerve, innervate cardiac muscle, smooth muscle, gland*
  - **Neuron**: *preganglionic, postganglionic*
  - **Fiber**: *general visceral efferent, general visceral afferent*
  - **System**: *sympathetic (thoracolumbar), parasympathetic (crabiosacral)*
  - **Neurotransmitter**
  
    - **Cholinergic (acetylcholine)**: *preganglionic, parasympathetic postglionic, sweat gland*
    - **Adrenergic (norepinephrine)**: *sympathetic postganglionic*

- **Sympathetic nervous system**

  - **Function**: 
  
    - **General**: *catabolic, flight-or-fight*
    - **Action**: *increase heart rate & dilate coronary artery, bronchodilation & vasoconstriction of pulmonary vessel*

  - **Component**

    - **Preganglionic cell body**: *lateral horn (intrmediolateral cell column) of spinal cord T1-L2*
    - **Preganglionic fiber**: *through white rami communicantes, enter sympathetic chain ganglion, synpase*
    - **Postganglionic fiber**: *through gray rami communicantes, join spinal nerve, supply blood vessel & hair follicle & sweat gland*

  - **Sympathetic trunk**

    - **Component**: *preganglionic sympathetic fiber (ascending or descending), cell body of postganglionic sympathetic fiber, viesecral afferent fiber*
    - **Course**: *descend in front of neck of rib, enter abdomen (through crus or medial arch)*
    - **Cervicothoracic (stellate) ganglion**: *fusion of inferior cervical ganglion & first thoracic ganglion*
    - **Branch**: *cardiac branch, pulmonary branch, mediastinal branch, splanchnic branch*
    - **Connection**: *with spinal nerve, through gray & while rami communicantes*

  - **Rami communicantes**

    - **White rami communicantes**

      - **Content**: 
      
        - **Preganglionic sympathetic GVE fiber**: *myelinated, cell body in lateral horn of spinal cord*
        - **GVA fiber**: *cell body in dorsal root ganglion*
    
      - **Connection**: *spinal nerve T1-L2*
    
    - **Gray rami communicantes**

      - **Content**

        - **Postganglionic sympathetic GVE**: *unmyelinated, cell body in sympathetic trunk, supply blood vessel & sweat gland & arrector pilli*
    
      - **Connection**: *every spinal nerve*

  - **Thoracic splanchnic nerve**

    - **Content**

      - **Preganglionic sympathetic GVE fiber**: *cell body in lateral horn of spinal cord*
      - **GVA fiber**: *cell body in dorsal root ganglion*

    - **Greater splanchnic nerve**

      - **Origin**: *5th-9th thoracic sympathetic ganglion*
      - **Coursse**: *pierce diaphragm through crus*
      - **End**: *celiac ganglion*

    - **Lesser splanchnic nerve**

      - **Origin**: *10th-11th thoracic sympathetic ganglion*
      - **Coursse**: *pierce diaphragm through crus*
      - **End**: *aorticorenal ganglion*

    - **Least splanchnic nerve**

      - **Origin**: *12th thoracic sympathetic ganglion*
      - **Coursse**: *pierce diaphragm through crus*
      - **End**: *renal plexus*

- **Parasympathetic nervous system**

  - **Function**

    - **General**: *anabolic*
    - **Distribution**: *not as widely as sympathetic, not supply body wall & extermity*
    - **Action**: *decrease heart rate & constrict coronary artery, bronchoconstriction & vasodilation of pulmonary vessel, increase secretion*

  - **Component**

    - **Cranial nerve III, VII, IX**: *preganglionic GVE fiber to cranial autonomic ganglia (ciliary, submandibular, pterygopalatine, otic)*
    - **Cranial nerve X (vagus)**: *preganglionic GVE fiber, GVA fiber, supply thoracic & abdominal viscera (except that supplied by pelvic splanchnic nerve)*
    - **Pelvic splanchnic nerve (S2-S4)**: *preganglionic GVE fiber to terminal ganglia, supply descending colon & sigmoid colon & pelvic viscera*

  - **Right vagus nerve**

    - **Right recurrent laryngeal nerve**: *loop around right subclavian artery, ascend in neck between trahcea & esophagus*
    - **Branch**: *to cariac pleuxs, pulmonary plexus, esophageal plexus*
    - **Posterior vagal trunk**: *posterior to esophagus, enter abdomen through esophageal hiatus*

  - **Left vagus nerve**

    - **Left recurrent laryngeal nerve**: *loop around arch or aorta (left to ligamentum arteriosum), ascend in neck between trahcea & esophagus*
    - **Branch**: *to cariac pleuxs, pulmonary plexus, esophageal plexus*
    - **Anterior vagal trunk**: *anterior to esophagus, enter abdomen through esophageal hiatus*
