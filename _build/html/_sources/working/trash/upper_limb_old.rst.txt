Upper Limb 
==========

Shoulder and Pectoral Region 
----------------------------

Bones 
^^^^^

- **Clavicle** 

  - S shape
  - Articulate with acromion and sternum 
  - **Conoid tubercle** and **trapezoid line**: attachment of coracoclavicular ligament

- **Scapula** 

  - Posterior surface:

    - **Spine**: divide **supraspinous fossa** and **infraspinous fossa** 
    - **Acromion**: articulate with clavicle 
    - **Greater scapular notch**

  - **Costal surface**: 
  
    - **Subscapular fossa**

  - Superior border:

    - **Coracoid process**: attachment of coracobrachialis, biceps brachii, pectoralis minor 
    - **Suprascapular notch**: passage of suprascapular nerve 

  - Lateral angle:

    - **Glenoid cavity**: articulate with humurus 

- **Humerus** (proximal)

  - **Head**: articulate with scapula 
  - **Neck**
    
    - **Anatomical neck**: between head and tubercles 
    - **Surgical neck**: between tubercles and shaft, posterior are axillary nerve and posterior circumflex humeral artery, common site of fractures

  - **Tubercles**:

    - **Grater tubercle**: attachment of supraspinatus, infraspinatus, teres minor 
    - **Lesser tubercle**: attachment of subscapularis 
    - **Intertubercular sulcus**: passage of long head of biceps brachii, have lateral and medial lips 
    - **Deltoid tuberosity**: attachment of deltiod
