Substrates for Gluconeogenesis
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- **Lactate**: *lactate, lactate dehydrogenase, pyruvate, Cori cycle*

- **Glycerol**: *glycerol, glycerol-3-phosphate, dihydroxyacetone phosphate*
