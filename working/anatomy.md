## Abdominal Wall

### Muscle of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **External oblique**

  - **Origin**: *lower ribs*
  - **Insertion**: *iliac crest, linea alba*
  - **Innervation**: *anterior rami of T7-T12*
  - **Function**: *compress abdomen, flex trunk*

- **Internal oblique**

  - **Origin**: *iliac crest, inguinal ligament*
  - **Insertion**: *lower ribs, pubic crest, linea alba*
  - **Innervation**: *anterior rami of T7-L1*
  - **Function**: *compress abdomen, flex trunk*
  
- **Transversus abdominis**

  - **Origin**: *iliac crest, inguinal ligament, lower costal cartilage*
  - **Insertion**: *pubic crest, linea alba*
  - **Innervation**: *anterior rami of T7-L1*
  - **Function**: *compress abdomen*

- **Rectus abdominis**

  - **Origin**: *pubic crest, pubic symphysis*
  - **Insertion**: *xiphoid process, lower costal cartilage*
  - **Innervation**: *anterior rami of T7-T12*
  - **Function**: *compress abdomen, flex trunk*

- **Pyramidalis**

  - **Origin**: *pubic symphysis*
  - **Insertion**: *linea alba*
  - **Innervation**: *anterior rami of T12*
  - **Function**: *tense linea alba*

### Fascia and Ligament of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Superfical fascia**

  - **Superficial (fatty) layer**: *Camper fascia*
  - **Deep (membranous) layer**: *Scarpa fascia, Colles fascia (perineum), Dartos fascia (scrotum), fascia lata (thigh)*

- **Deep fascia**: *cover abdominal muscle*
- **Rectus sheath**: *aponeuorosis of abdominal muscle*

  - **Above arcuate line**

    - **Anterior layer**: *external oblique, internal oblique*
    - **Posterior layer**: *internal oblique, transversus abdominus*

  - **Below arcuate line**

    - **Anterior layer**: *external oblique, internal oblique, transversus abdominus*
    - **Posterior layer**: *none, contact with transversalis fascia*

- **Ligament**

  - **Linea alba**: *tendinous median raphe, xiphoid process to pubic sympysis, linea nigra (pregnancy)*
  - **Linea semilunaris**: *lateral border of rectus abdominus*
  - **Linea semicircularis (arcuate line)**: *inferior limit of poeterior layer of rectus sheath*
  - **Lacunar ligament**: *medial border of femoral ring*
  - **Pectineal ligament**: *along pectineal line of pubis*
  - **Inguinal ligament**: *lower border of external oblique, anterior superior iliac spine to pubic tubercle*
  - **Iliopectineal ligament**: *separate muscular & vascular lacuna (deep to inguinal ligament)*
  - **Conjoint tendon**: *extension of internal oblique aponeurosis, to pubic tubercle*

### Inguinal Region

- **Inguinal triangle (Hesselbach)**

  - **Medial**: *linea semilunaris (rectus abdominis)*
  - **Lateral**: *inferior epigastric vessel*
  - **Inferior**: *inguinal ligament*

- **Inguinal ring**: *start and end of inguinal canal*

  - **Superficial inguinal ring**: *aponeurosis of external oblique, lateral to pubic tubercle*
  - **Deep inguinal ring**: *transversalis fascia, lateral to inferior epigastric vessel*

- **Inguinal canal**: *transmit spermatic cord, round ligament of uterus, ilioinguinal nerve (not in spermatic cord)*

  - **Anterior wall**: *aponeurosis of external oblique*
  - **Posterior wall**: *transversalis fascia*
  - **Superior wall**: *border of internal oblique & transversus abdominus*
  - **Inferior wall**: *inguinal ligament & lacunar ligament*

- **Inguinal hernia**: *protrusion of intestine, above inguinal ligament*

  - **Indirect inguinal hernia**: *through inguinal canal to scrotum, lateral to inferior epigastric vessel, congenital*
  - **Direct inguinal hernia**: *through inguinal triangle, medial to inferior epigastric vessel, acquired*

### Spermatic Cord & Testis

- **Spermatic cord**

  - **Composition**: *ductus deferens, testicular artery, testicular vein (pampinoform plexus), genital femoral nerve (genital branch), lymph vessel*
  - **Fascia**

    - **External spermatic fascia**: *from external oblique*
    - **Cremasteric muscle & fascia**: *from internal oblique, innervated by genitofemoral nerve*
    - **Internal spermatic fascia**: *from transversalis fascia*

- **Fetal structure**

  - **Processus vaginalis testis**

    - **Peritoneal diverticulum**: *into scrotum, close to form tunica vaginalis*
    - **Persist**: *cause indirect inguinal hernia, fluid accumulation*

  - **Tunica vaginalis**: *double serous membrane, cover testis & epididymis*
  - **Gubernaculum testis**: *ligament from testis to scrotum, testicular descent, homologous to ovarian & round ligament of uterus*

- **Scrotum**

  - **Consist**: *pigmented skin, dartos fascia & smooth muscle (wrinkle to regulate temprature)*
  - **Innervation**: *genitofemoral nerve, ilioinguinal nerve, perineal nerve, posterior fermoral cutaneos nerve*
  - **Vasculature**: *external pudendal artery, internal pudendal artery*
  - **Lymphatics**: *superficial inguinal node*

- **Testis**

  - **Innervation**: *autonomic nerve*
  - **Vasculature**: *testicular artery, testicular vein (right to IVC, left to renal vein)*
  - **Lymphatics**: *deep inguinal node*

### Inner Surface of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Fossa**

  - **Supravesical fossa**: *between median & medial umbilical fold*
  - **Medial inguinal fossa**: *between medial & lateral umbilical fold*
  - **Lateral inguinal fossa**: *lateral to lateral umbilical fold*

- **Umbilical fold (ligament)**

  - **Median umbilical fold**: *remnant of urachus*
  - **Medial umbilical fold**: *remnant of umbilical artery*
  - **Lateral umbilical fold**: *cover inferior epigastric vessel*

- **Transversalis fascia**: *between parietal peritoneum & abdominal muscle, form femoral sheath & internal spermatic fascia*

### Nerve of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Subcostal nerve**

  - **Course**: *from T12, innervate abdominal muscle*
  - **Lateral & anterior branch**: *supply skin of lateral hip & lower abdomen*

- **Iliohypogastric nerve**

  - **Course**: *from L1, innervate abdominal muscle*
  - **Lateral & anterior branch**: *supply skin of lateral buttock & above pubis*

- **Ilioinguinal nerve**

  - **Course**: *from L1, innervate abdominal muscle, pierce internal oblique, through inguinal canal, exit superficial inguinal ring*
  - **Femoral branch**: *supply upper medial anterior thigh*
  - **Anterior scrotal nerve**: *supply root of penis, anterior scrotum*

### Blood Vessel of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Superior epigastric artery**: 

  - **Course**: *from internal thoracic artery, descend behind rectus abdominus*

- **Inferior epigastric artery**

  - **Course**: *from external iliac artery, ascend behind rectus abdominus, anastomoses with superior epigastric artery*

  - **Cremasteric artery**: *accompany spermatic cord*

- **Superficial epigastric artery**

  - **Course**: *from femoral artery, run superiorly toward umbilicus*

- **Deep circumflex iliac artery**: 

  - **Course**: *from external iliac artery, run superiorly between internal oblique & transversus abdominus, anastomoses with musculophrenic artery*

- **Superficial circumflex iliac artery**

  - **Course**: *from femoral artery, run superiorly laterally*

- **Superficial pudendal artery**

  - **Course**: *from femoral artery, run medially toward pubis*

### Lymphatic of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Axillary lymph node**: *region above umbilicus*
- **Superficial lumph node**: *region below umbilicus*

  - **Drain to**: *external iliac node, lumbar node*

### Surface Anatomy of Anterior Abdominal Wall

- **Division 1**: *left & right hypogastric, epigastric, left & right lumbar, umbilical, left & right inguinal, hypogastric*
- **Division 2**: *left upper quadrant, right upper quadrant, left lower quadrant, right lower quadrant*
- **Umbilicus**: *level of L3-L4 vertebra, dermatome T10*

## Peritoneal Cavity

### Peritoneum

- **Parietal peritoneum**: *line abdominal wall & pelvic wall & diaphragm*, innervated by somatic nerve
- **Visceral peritoneum**: *cover viscera, innervated by visceral nerve, insensitive to pain*
- **Peritoneal reflection**: *support viscera, contain neurovascular structure*

### Peritoneal Reflection

- **Omentum**

  - **Lesser omentum**: 

    - **Position**: *from liver (porta hepatis) to stomach (lesser curvature), anterior wall of lesser sac*
    - **Vessel**: *left & right gastric vessel*
    - **Right margin**: *contain proper hepatic artery, bile duct, portal vein*
    - **Ligament**: *hepatogastric ligament, hepatoduodenal ligament*

  - **Greater omentum**

    - **Position**: *hang down from greater curvature of stomach, cover abdominal viscera*
    - **Vessel**: *left & right gastroepiplioc vessel*
    - **Ligament**

      - **Gastrosplenic ligament**: *from stomach to spleen, contain short gastric & left gastroepiploic vessel*
      - **Splenorenal ligament**: *from spleen to kidney, contain splenic vessel*
      - **Gastrophrenic ligament**: *from stomach to diaphragm*
      - **Gastrocolic ligament**: *from stomach to transverse colon*

- **Mesentery**

  - **Mesemtery of small intestine**

    - **Position**: *suspend jejunum & ileum to posterior abdominal wall*
    - **Vessel**: *jejunal & ileal vessel*

  - **Transverse mesocolon**

    - **Position**: *suspend transverse colon to posterior abdominal wall*
    - **Vessel**: *middle colic vessel*

  - **Sigmoid mesocolon**

    - **Position**: *suspend sigmoid colon to posterior abdominal wall*
    - **Vessel**: *sigmoid vessel*

  - **Mesoappendix**

    - **Position**: *suspend appendix to ileum*
    - **Vessel**: *appendicular vessel*

- **Other peritoneal ligament**

  - **Phrenicocolic ligament**: *from left colic flexure to diaphragm*
  - **Falciform ligament**: *connect liver to diaphragm & anterior abdominal wall, contain ligamentum teres hepatis & paraumbilical vein (from umbilicus to portal vein)*  
  - **Ligamentum teres hepatis (round ligament of liver)**: *remnant of left umbilical vein, from umbilicus to liver (inferior surface)*
  - **Coronary ligament**: *from liver (diaphragmatic surface) to diaphragm, extend as left & right triangle ligament*
  - **Ligamentum venosum**: *remnant of ductus venosus, in fissure of liver (inferior surface)*

### Peritoneal Cavity

- **Lesser sac (omental bursa)**

  - **Position**: *behind liver & lesser omentum & stomach*
  - **Recess**: *superior recess, inferior recess, splenic recess*

- **Greater sac**

  - **Position**: *most peritoneal cavity*
  - **Recess**

    - **Subphrenic (suprahepatic) recess**: *between diaphragm & liver*
    - **Subhepatic (hepatorenal) recess**: *between liver & kidney*
    - **Paracolic recess (gutter)**: *lateral to ascending & descending colon*

- **Epiploic (omental) foramen**: 

  - **Position**: *opening between lesser & greater sac*
  - **Boundary**: *superior: liver (caudate lobe), inferior: duodenum (first part), anterior: portal triad, posterior: IVC*
